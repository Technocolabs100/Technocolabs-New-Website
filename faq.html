Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Technocolabs?

    We are a software development company specialized in building software from scratch to a comprehensive product. We are a team of talented developers with both deep experience and strong skills in making web applications. We cooperate mainly with E-commerce, Marketplace, FinTech, SaaS and AdTech clients.

  • What are your main technologies?

    We build software in Python, Java, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, microservices architecture, Elixir, REST API, WebSockets, JavaScript, Vue.js, React, React Native, Redis, ELK Stack, RabbitMQ, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, nginx, Amazon Web Services: EC2, S3, CloudFront. We have on board experienced team of UI/UX designers as well, who take care of appearance and intuitiveness of our developed projects.

  • I want to create a new product. Why should I choose Technocolabs to work with?

    Because we are a reliable partner who focuses on making high-quality software. We are rapidly growing as a Ruby and JavaScript software development company focused that has global experience with cooperation such companies like Mittmedia (the largest media group in Sweden), Kesko Senukai Digital (an e-commerce company, operating online stores in the Baltic market) or Yieldbird (leading provider of the yield-optimization services).

  • What details should I provide in a project specification?

    There are no specific guidelines. All you have to do is tell us what product you want to build and what functionalities you need. Anyway, we highly recommend to clearly define what exactly you plan on building, or how to improve your existing web or mobile product. We offer a complimentary project specification template for all of our clients. If you need it, contact us on: technocolabs@gmail.com

  • What is an MVP?

    MVP (Minimum Viable Product) is a product with just enough features to satisfy early customers and to provide feedback for future product development. In other words, it is a product built as quickly as possible with enough basic functionalities. This approach allows the implementation of a product on the market so the users can test it, you can gather feedback, and constantly improve your product. MVP provides cost-effectiveness and time savings.
